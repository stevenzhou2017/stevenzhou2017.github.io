MR. Zhou Junyang has been working on his PhD in Department of Computer Science, HKBU after he received his M.Sc of Probability and Mathematic Statistics in Sun Yat-Sen University 2003. He also recieved his B.Sc of Applied Mathematics in Department of Mathematics , Sun Yat-Sen University 2000.
MR. Zhou Junyang's general research interests are wireless location, mobile/pervasive computing and Real-Time network. His supervisor is Dr. Joseph Kee-Yin, NG , and Dr. Jianliang Xu is his co-supervisor. Mr. Zhou Junyang is good at modelling and algorithm analysis. Now he is working on the research about mobile station location.
